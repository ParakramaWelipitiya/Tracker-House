Privacy Policy

Tracker House values your privacy and strives to maintain transparency in how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of our app.

Information Collection

Currently, Tracker House does not collect any personal data. In the future, we may collect the following information for business purposes:

  • Usernames and email addresses for communication and updates.
  • Optional feedback submitted by users to improve app features.
  • Usage analytics (anonymous) to enhance app performance and explore trends.

No payment or purchase information is collected, as bikes can only be purchased at our physical dealerships.

How We Use Your Information

  • Send relevant updates, promotions, and news regarding our bikes and dealership events.
  • Improve the app experience by analyzing trends and feedback.
  • Ensure communication with users for business purposes only.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data you provided through the app.
  • Request deletion of your personal data at any time.
  • Opt-out from receiving future email communications.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Dealership Visits

All bikes showcased in the app are for exploration purposes only. Users cannot purchase bikes directly through the app. To purchase any bike, users must visit one of our dealerships. Dealership locations and contact details are available within the app.

Third-Party Services

If we integrate third-party services or analytics tools in the future, they may collect certain information according to their privacy policies. We will ensure transparency regarding any such integrations.
